Welcome to

AUP Lectures Resources Management System.

Here, you'll find everything you need for your studies: lectures, notes, course outlines, assignments, and important notices. It's your go-to place for academic resources, where teachers, class representatives (CRs), and students can easily share and access course materials. With efficient communication and collaboration, we're here to help you succeed in your academic journey.
